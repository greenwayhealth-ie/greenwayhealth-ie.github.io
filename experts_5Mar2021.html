Specializing in GaitScanTM prescription orthotics (insoles) www.tog.com

Prescription orthotics are used in the treatment and prevention of foot dysfunction, shin-splints, over-pronation, fallen arches, metatarsalgia, diabetes, knee, hip and lower back problems. Wide variety of personalised orthotics suitable for everyday footwear, sports shoes, work boots, wellies
etc.

A prescription orthotic is a device designed to align the foot and ankle into the most anatomically efficient position. They look like insoles, but are biomechanical medical appliances that are custom made to correct your specific foot imbalance. Custom orthotics work on your feet much like glasses work on your eyes – they reduce stress and strain on your body by bringing your feet back into proper alignment. Custom orthotics fit into your shoes as comfortably as an insole – and they have the advantage of having been made from precise imprints of your feet.
